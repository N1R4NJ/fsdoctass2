    FULLY ELECTRIC M POWER.

  • The BMW i7 M70 xDrive is the most powerful BMW M model with a fully electric drive system. It is equipped with an electric motor at both the front and rear axle generating a maximum system output of 485 kW (660 hp)*. An M specific configuration enables the rear-axle motor, in particular, to achieve unprecedented levels of power density. The drive unit has a six-phase design with a dual inverter. This forms the basis for a very substantial increase in peak output that remains on tap up to high speeds, endowing the BMW i7 M70 xDrive with the power delivery characteristic of M models.
